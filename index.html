Biography

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer and Information Systems from the University of Aizu, Japan, in 2022. Prior to that, I received my Master's degree in Computer and Information Systems from the University of Aizu, Japan, in 2019, and my Bachelor's degree in Network Engineering from Henan University, China, in 2014. I am currently an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Hainan University since April 2022.
My research interests include Machine Learning, Signal Processing, and Seamless Monitoring of Physiological Signals.

Accepting Students: I am looking for highly motivated students, feel free to drop me an email with your CV.
